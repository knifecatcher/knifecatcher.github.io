# Meta Ray-Ban Display
<br>
Meta Ray-Ban Display and the Meta Neural Band are a huge leap forward in wearable tech.<br><br>
-High resolution built in display that's there when you want it and gone when you don't<br>
-Messages and quick replies that you can send with a simple hand movement<br>
-Live captions that allow you to subtitle your conversations and translate into your preferred language<br>
-Photos that you can preview (and zoom!)<br>
-Meta AI with visual response - all without needing to pull out your phone<br>
